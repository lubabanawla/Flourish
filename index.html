Flourish is a free, one-day hackathon in Philadelphia for middle and high school girls and gender-nonconforming youth interested in coding and technology. Hosted by Hack Club at Launchpad, it features hands-on workshops, creative team challenges, and opportunities to make new friends. All skill levels are welcome for a day of learning and fun.

FAQs

  • Where is the event?

    801 Market St Suite 301, Philadelphia, PA 19107

  • When is the event?

    Saturday, June 14th | 9 AM - 4 PM

  • Who can attend?

    This event is for all teenagers aged 13–17 who are interested in coding!

  • What if I have never coded before?

    No experience, no problem! This event is beginner-friendly and we’ll have workshops and mentors to help you.

  • How much does it cost to attend?

    Nothing — the event is completely free.

  • What should I bring?

    Just your laptop and yourself! If you don’t have access to a laptop, please indicate that on the registration form as early as possible.

  • What do I do if I have more questions?

    Feel free to reach out. You can send Zenab an email at zenab@hackclub.com
